Genes for Change KE is a youth-led initiative designed to
bridge the gap in genetics and molecular biology education and
awareness among Kenyan youth, particularly in underserved communities.

What we do

Hands on Education & Experimental learning

we design and deliver interactive workshops,science camps and mobile lab experiences where teens can engage with real lab techniques(e.g DNA eaxtraction,PCR simulations, electrophoresis ) these programs align with and enrich the national science curriculum but go beyond to foster curiosity,creativity,and problem solving in biology. we collaborate with schools,universities and STEM hubs to make this accesible and scalable

Outreach & Awareness campaign

through community forums school talks and digital media,we raise awareness on crucial genetics topics such as: hereditary disease and genetic counselling the role of DNA in injustice and identity ethical issues in biotechnology and genetic engineering We use youth-friendly formats (e.g., storytelling, art, games, and media) to make genetics approachable and relevant to daily life.

Youth Leadership and career exposure

We empower young people as Genetics Ambassadors â€” trained peer educators who lead awareness and learning activities in their own schools and communities. We host career panels, mentorship events, and lab visits to expose teens to opportunities in biomedical research, bioinformatics, public health, and agrigenomics. This encourages long-term engagement with life sciences and helps build a pipeline of African scientists and innovators.
