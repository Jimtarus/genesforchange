Our Mission

At Genes for Change, we empower young minds across Kenya to unlock the transformative potential of genetics and molecular biology.

Education

We deliver hands-on genetics and molecular biology education through school workshops, virtual labs, and community STEM clubs. We also support teachers with resources and training.

Innovation

We inspire youth to think like scientists and innovators. From student research projects to national biotech hackathons, we nurture curiosity and critical thinking.

Awareness

We run campaigns, podcasts, and school talks to demystify genetics and build public understanding of how DNA science touches everyday life.

Inclusion

We prioritize equity by focusing on underserved schools and promoting girls in STEM. Every voice and every brain matters.

From Curiosity to Breakthroughs

In a rural school outside Kisumu, a 16-year-old student once asked during a workshop, "Can DNA explain why crops fail even with enough rain?" That curiosity led her to a mentorship program where she explored plant genetics and eventually presented her findings at a national science fair.

Stories like this fuel our work. Because when young people discover that the science of genes can answer their everyday questions, it becomes personal. Powerful. Possible.
