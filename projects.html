Welcome to Genes for Change

Our mission is to inspire and empower the youth of Kenya by providing them with access to genetics and molecular biology education. Through hands-on learning experiences and innovative resources, we aim to foster curiosity and knowledge in the next generation of scientists, health professionals, and innovators.

Our Visionary Projects

Starting Genetics Clubs in Schools

We are working on establishing **Genetics and Molecular Biology Clubs** in high schools across Kenya. These clubs will serve as hubs for students to learn about DNA, genetic research, and the fascinating world of molecular biology. Through weekly activities, students will engage in hands-on projects, educational talks, and discussions with guest speakers from universities and research institutions.

  • Key Features: Weekly interactive sessions, expert guest talks, hands-on experiments.
  • Timeline: Ongoing initiative, with plans to launch in 2025.
Learn More

DIY Genetics Kits for Students

We are creating **DIY Genetics Kits** that allow students to conduct simple experiments at home. These kits will include materials to extract DNA from fruit, test for genetic traits, and understand basic molecular biology principles. Our goal is to make genetics accessible, fun, and easy for young minds across Kenya, even in areas where laboratory resources are limited.

  • Key Features: Hands-on DIY kits, instructional guides, accessible to all students.
  • Timeline: Expected rollout in 2025 after prototype testing.
Learn More

Social Media Campaign: Genetics for All

We are launching a **Social Media Campaign** aimed at raising awareness about genetics, molecular biology, and its importance in everyday life. The campaign will feature short educational videos, fun facts, and interactive content to engage youth on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter. By using AI tools and digital engagement strategies, we will reach a broader audience and ignite interest in genetics.

  • Key Features: Educational videos, interactive posts, fun facts about genetics.
  • Timeline: Launching Q4 2024, ongoing.
